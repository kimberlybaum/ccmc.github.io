The Concrete Cowboy MetaCollective

Solana City’s Art Community

The Concrete Cowboy MetaCollective is an MMORPG powered by the Solana Blockchain, and is the first project to be part of the Solana City metaverse. The first residents of the growing Solana City, and the exclusive members of the CCMC, are the titular Concrete Cowboys. As part of the CCMC, you will have the unique ability to showcase your NFTs in your virtual home, regardless of which blockchain they live on. A leaderboard will show who has the top NFT collection, in terms of its popularity within the community. Players will also be able to explore Solana City’s bars and clubs, play board games with friends in coffee shops, and vie for rare airdrops by participating in fun, unique in-game competitions. Communities of real-world creators will host NFT music releases in the Collective’s clubs and NFT visual art exhibits in its communal galleries.

  • Proudly display your NFTs, whether they're on Solana, Ethereum, or FLOW
  • Create and mint visual art and music NFTs in-game in the Collective's Studio
  • Attend live performances by real-world musicians in Solana City's virtual clubs
  • Visit showings for NFT artists debuing new lines of work in the Collective's galleries
  • Participate in members-only competitions to win rare airdrops

The Concrete Cowboys

Your ticket to the Metaverse

As the iconic cowboy is to the American west, so are these adorable urbanites to the vibrant city they inhabit. All at the vanguard of the art scene in Solana City, they’ve joined to form the Concrete Cowboy MetaCollective, a close-knit group of NFT collectors and creators, in order to appreciate others’ art, showcase their own, and party with friends old & new. Each lives in a stylish flat, where they host parties to show off their decor and display their art collections. Sometimes they enjoy Solana City’s nightlife a little too much, but they never return home without a new connection and a deeper place in their community.
