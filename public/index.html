The CCMC Metaverse

Solana City’s Art Community

The Concrete Cowboy Metaverse is an MMO RPG powered by the Solana Blockchain, with each Concrete Cowboy providing entry to this virtual world. Our world provides the unique ability to showcase your NFTs purchased by any cryptocurrency, in your own MetaCollective apartment. Users are able to showcase NFTs that we verify are owned by them, so you know you’re seeing the originals. The goal of the project will be to develop a diverse community of people united by a common enthusiasm for blockchain technology and art, allowing them to form new connections and grow their community.

  • Top the leaderboard by having the most visted gallery
  • Create and mint art and music in-game
  • Attend live performances by up-and coming artists in Virtual Clubs
  • Participate in in-game only airdrops

The Concrete Cowboys

Your ticket to the Metaverse

As the iconic cowboy is to the American west, so are these adorable urbanites to the vibrant city they inhabit. All at the vanguard of the art scene in Solana City, they’ve joined to form the Concrete Cowboy MetaCollective, a close-knit group of NFT collectors and creators, in order to appreciate others’ art, showcase their own, and party with friends old & new. Each lives in a stylish flat, where they host parties to show off their decor and display their art collections. Sometimes they enjoy Solana City’s nightlife a little too much, but they never return home without a new connection and a deeper place in their community.
