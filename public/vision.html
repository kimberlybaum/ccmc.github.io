    Our Vision

    We grew up playing Club Penguin, Webkinz, and RuneScape. As adults, we’ve been fascinated by blockchain technology and all its potential to revolutionize globally fundamental systems. We found that as we collected NFTs on the Solana blockchain over the last few months, we didn’t have an outlet to meet with friends, display our Solana NFTs, look at friends’ collections, and bond with new people over our shared enthusiasm. We plan to provide an exclusive platform to act as a hub for NFT appreciation, sharing, exchange, and donation just for fanatics like us. This project will be the first step in a larger plan we have, to develop the Solana City metaverse on top of the Solana blockchain. We will work to build a strong community and a complex world around it, making it more than just a set of galleries to walk around in. The world will also have games, coffee shops, bars, clubs, and galleries sponsored by the Collective itself. Gain a following of people who appreciate your NFTs, and climb the leaderboard as you accumulate a stronger and stronger collection. Conceptualize, create, and mint your own new NFTs with friends in the Collective’s art studio. Participate in members-only events to vie for rare airdrops & merch.

    We also realized we didn’t want to exclude people with Ethereum or FLOW NFTs from fully showcasing their collections to the community. That’s why we’re building cross-platform NFT recognition, so that you can hang not only your Solana art on the wall, but all your art. This will provide a richer, more accessible experience to a larger portion of the blockchain community.

    We’ve begun assembling a team with professional experience in artistic design, blockchain and web development, game design, operations, and community outreach. We want to get even more people on board to bring our ambitious vision to life, and we’ll reinvest our profits from the initial drop into that effort.
